Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Deprem Alarm. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your personal data.

✅ Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

1. Data We Use

  • Location Data: Used only when the Distance Filter feature is enabled. Stored locally on your device only, never transmitted to any server.
  • Phone Numbers: Entered for Family Safety Mode, stored locally only, never shared with third parties.
  • App Settings: All preferences stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identity information (name, email, etc.)
  • Financial information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

No user data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.

3. Earthquake Data

Earthquake data is fetched from the Kandilli Observatory (KOERI) public API. This data is used solely for real-time earthquake monitoring and is not stored on your device.

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera: Controls rear flash for Silent Scream feature. No images captured.
  • Vibration: For earthquake alarm and SOS vibration signals.
  • SMS: For Family Safety Mode automatic alerts to your contacts.
  • Location: Optional, for Distance Filter feature only.
  • Internet: To fetch earthquake data from Kandilli API.
  • Wake Lock: To keep device awake during active alarm.
  • System Alert Window: To display the animated ring overlay.
  • Foreground Service: For continuous background earthquake monitoring.

5. Survival Signal Feature

The ultrasonic frequency, rescue signal, and SOS vibration features are designed to assist users trapped under debris.

⚠️ This is an auxiliary tool. Always contact emergency services (112, AFAD) in an emergency.

6. Data Security

All data is stored exclusively on your device. No cloud storage or remote servers are used. Uninstalling the app deletes all data automatically.

7. Children's Privacy

Deprem Alarm does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for all age groups.

8. Third-Party Services

No third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services are used. The only external connection is to the Kandilli Observatory public API.

9. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated via app updates. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For questions, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.
