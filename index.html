Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Deprem Alarm. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your personal data.

✅ Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

1. Data We Use

  • Location Data: Used only when the Distance Filter feature is enabled. Your GPS location is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to any server.
  • Phone Numbers: Phone numbers entered for the Family Safety Mode are stored locally on your device only and are never shared with third parties.
  • App Settings: All preferences (alarm threshold, sound settings, animation mode, sleep mode, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Deprem Alarm does NOT collect:

  • Personal identity information (name, email, etc.)
  • Financial information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Crash reports sent to external servers

No user data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.

3. Earthquake Data

The app fetches earthquake data from the Kandilli Observatory and Earthquake Research Institute (KOERI), which is a publicly available API. This data is used solely for real-time earthquake monitoring and is not stored on your device.

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera Permission: Used for the Silent Scream feature to control the rear camera flash (torch). No images or videos are captured or stored.
  • Vibration Permission: Used for earthquake alarm vibrations and SOS vibration signals.
  • SMS Permission: Used for the Family Safety Mode to send automatic SMS alerts to your pre-defined contacts during an earthquake. SMS content is sent only to numbers you specify.
  • Location Permission: Used optionally for the Distance Filter feature to alert you only about earthquakes near your location.
  • Internet Permission: Used to fetch earthquake data from the Kandilli Observatory API.
  • Wake Lock Permission: Used to keep the device awake during an active earthquake alarm.
  • System Alert Window: Used to display the animated ring overlay around your camera cutout on top of other apps.
  • Foreground Service: Used to run earthquake monitoring in the background continuously.

5. Survival Signal Feature

The Survival Signal feature (ultrasonic frequency for rescue dogs, 2kHz rescue signal, SOS vibration) is designed to assist users trapped under debris after an earthquake.

⚠️ This feature is an auxiliary tool and does not replace official emergency services. Always contact emergency services (112, AFAD) in case of emergency.

6. Data Security

All application data is stored exclusively on your device. No cloud storage or remote servers are used. When you uninstall the application, all data is automatically deleted from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Deprem Alarm does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. The application is designed to be safe for all age groups.

8. Third-Party Services

The application does not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The only external connection is to the Kandilli Observatory public API for earthquake data.

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through application updates. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.
