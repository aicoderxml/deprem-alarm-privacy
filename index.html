Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Deprem Alarm. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your personal data.

✅ Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.
💳 In-App Purchase Notice: Deprem Alarm is free to download. It offers an optional, one-time in-app purchase ("Deprem Alarm Pro") that unlocks additional features. All purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

1. Data We Use

  • Location Data: Used only when the Distance Filter feature is enabled. Stored locally on your device only, never transmitted to any server.
  • Phone Numbers: Entered for Family Safety Mode, stored locally only, never shared with third parties.
  • App Settings: All preferences stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identity information (name, email, etc.)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

No user data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.

3. Earthquake Data

Earthquake data is fetched from two public sources: the Kandilli Observatory (KOERI) API for earthquakes in and around Turkey, and the United States Geological Survey (USGS) public API for worldwide earthquake data. Both sources are used solely for real-time earthquake monitoring and are not stored on your device. The USGS-based "World Earthquakes" feature is optional and only active when explicitly enabled by the user.

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera: Controls rear flash for Silent Scream feature. No images captured.
  • Vibration: For earthquake alarm and SOS vibration signals.
  • SMS: For Family Safety Mode automatic alerts to your contacts.
  • Location: Optional, for Distance Filter feature only.
  • Internet: To fetch earthquake data from Kandilli API.
  • Wake Lock: To keep device awake during active alarm.
  • System Alert Window: To display the animated ring overlay.
  • Foreground Service: For continuous background earthquake monitoring.
  • Accessibility Service (Optional): Used exclusively to display the animated alarm ring on top of the lock screen during an active earthquake alert. This service does not read, record, or transmit any screen content, keystrokes, or other data. It can be enabled or disabled at any time from the app's Settings screen.

5. Survival Signal Feature

The ultrasonic frequency, rescue signal, and SOS vibration features are designed to assist users trapped under debris.

⚠️ This is an auxiliary tool. Always contact emergency services (112, AFAD) in an emergency.

6. Payment & Billing

Deprem Alarm is free to download and offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Deprem Alarm Pro") to unlock additional features. All payment transactions are handled exclusively by Google Play Billing under their payment policies. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or billing data.

7. Data Security

All data is stored exclusively on your device. No cloud storage or remote servers are used. Uninstalling the app deletes all data automatically.

8. Children's Privacy

Deprem Alarm does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for all age groups.

9. Third-Party Services

No third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services are used. The only external connections are to the Kandilli Observatory (KOERI) public API and the United States Geological Survey (USGS) public API, both used exclusively to fetch earthquake data. Neither service receives any personal data from the app — no identifiers, location, or usage data are sent as part of these requests.

10. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated via app updates. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For questions, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.

Her iki kaynak da yalnızca gerçek zamanlı deprem izleme amacıyla kullanılır ve cihazınızda saklanmaz. USGS tabanlı "Dünya Depremleri" özelliği isteğe bağlıdır ve yalnızca kullanıcı tarafından açıkça etkinleştirildiğinde çalışır.

  • Konum: İsteğe bağlı, yalnızca Mesafe Filtresi için.
  • İnternet: Kandilli API'sinden deprem verisi çekmek için.
  • Uyandırma Kilidi: Aktif alarm sırasında cihazı uyanık tutmak için.
  • Sistem Uyarı Penceresi: Animasyonlu halka katmanını göstermek için.
  • Ön Plan Servisi: Sürekli arka plan izlemesi için.
  • Erişilebilirlik Servisi (İsteğe Bağlı): Yalnızca aktif bir deprem uyarısı sırasında animasyonlu alarm halkasının kilit ekranının üzerinde gösterilebilmesi için kullanılır. Bu servis ekran içeriğini, tuş vuruşlarını veya başka herhangi bir veriyi okumaz, kaydetmez veya iletmez. Uygulamanın Ayarlar ekranından istediğiniz zaman açılıp kapatılabilir.
